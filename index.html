Could your sore throat be strep?

Your throat is itchy and scratchy. You find it painful to swallow, and your lymph nodes seem to be swollen. You may not have a fever, but you know something isn't right. Could your sore throat be a sign of strep (Group A Streptococcus)? Answer a few short questions to find out!
